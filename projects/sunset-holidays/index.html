How does Sunset Holidays operate?


Sunset Holidays works as the middle-man between clients and travel companies.
We are 100% committed to finding the best deals and planning the perfect trip.
We book anything including Flights, Accommodation, Transfers, Vehicle hires and Tours.
We also offer free travel advice about Safety, Entry Requirements, Health, Terrorism, Natural Disasters and Local Laws.
Using Sunset Holidays saves you hours of research time and while utilising our experience and knowledge in travel and planning.
Clients describe their travel plans and will be given various accommodation, flight and itinerary options based on their chosen travel.
We charge 8% of the total travel cost.
This covers detailed planning, booking and research hours.
*Clients must book their own travel insurance.
*Clients must also organise getting their own visa if required.
*Sunset Holidays is not responsible for any unexpected travel problems including flight delays.

We always book with trustworthy companies and operators based on reviews and experience. Sometimes an offer is too good to be true. You will have your own complete itinerary including flights, destinations, prices, reference numbers, confirmation numbers etc. Itinerary timetables are also optional.
